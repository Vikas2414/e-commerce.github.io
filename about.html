About Us

As we have it in our tagline, we're "For the gamers, by the gamers".it all started in 2014, a time when gaming industry was emerging but quality gaming accssories weren't available for affordable rates in India. people had to internationally purchase from other stores which used to cost them a fortune. Thus two young men in their 20s Devansh Katheria and Vikas Kumar Sethiya decided to take things in their hands.The beggining of Tech cart was small like any other ambitious bussiness and we had a single store selling computer accessories. But with our hardwork and your support we have 3 stores in Mumbai and around 50,000 annual buyers.
